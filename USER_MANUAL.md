# Gyan Setu — Step-by-Step User Manual

Complete walkthrough: signing up and using every feature, section by section, for every role.

---

## 1. Sign Up & Sign In

1. Go to the platform homepage.
2. Click **Sign Up** (new user) or **Sign In** (existing user).
3. Sign up with email/password or a supported social login.
4. Verify your email if asked.
5. After signing in, you land on `/dashboard`.
6. The platform checks your role and sends you to the right area automatically:
   - No role yet → **Student** area
   - `volunteer` → **Volunteer** area
   - `earc_staff` → **Staff (EARC)** area
   - `admin` / `super_admin` → **Admin** area
7. From then on, every sign-in takes you straight back to your dashboard.

---

## 2. Student — Step by Step

**Home:** Student Dashboard — quick overview of your tours, tests, and forms.

### 2.1 Tours
1. Open **Tours** from the sidebar.
2. Browse the list of available tours.
3. Click a tour to view details (dates, description, eligibility).
4. Click **Apply** to register your interest.
5. Track your application status on the same page.

### 2.2 Tests
1. Open **Tests**.
2. See tests assigned to you (linked to the tours you applied for).
3. Click a test to open it.
4. Answer questions — multiple choice, multi-select, or written.
5. Submit the test.
6. Objective (MCQ/multi-select) questions are scored instantly.
7. Written answers are graded later by an admin.
8. Return to **Tests** anytime to check your score/status.

### 2.3 Forms
1. Open **Forms**.
2. See any forms assigned to you (applications, consent forms, etc.).
3. Click a form to open it.
4. Fill in the fields (text, dropdowns, dates, file/image uploads as required).
5. Submit — you'll see a confirmation.

### 2.4 Profile
1. Open **Profile**.
2. View/update your personal details.
3. Save changes.

## 3. Volunteer — Step by Step

You become a volunteer once selected/assigned. All items below appear in the volunteer sidebar.

### 3.1 Tours
1. Open **Tours** to see tours you're assigned to.
2. Click a tour for details, dates, and your assigned group.

### 3.2 Groups
1. Open **Groups** to see your group members and group details.

### 3.3 Daily Log
1. Open **Daily Log**.
2. Click to add a new entry for the day.
3. Describe activities conducted.
4. Submit — repeat once per day during the tour.

### 3.4 Tour Report
1. Open **Tour Report**.
2. Fill in the end-of-tour summary form.
3. Submit for admin review.

### 3.5 Media
1. Open **Media**.
2. Upload photos/files from the tour.
3. Uploaded media appears in the shared gallery.

### 3.6 Expenses
1. Open **Expenses**.
2. Add an expense entry (amount, category, description, receipt if needed).
3. Submit for admin/finance review.

### 3.7 Travel
1. Open **Travel**.
2. Enter your travel details (mode, dates, tickets).
3. Submit.

### 3.8 Registration Fee
1. Open **Registration Fee**.
2. View your fee status and payment details.

### 3.9 Demo Evaluations
1. Open **Demo Evaluations**.
2. View/complete any evaluation assigned to you before a tour.

### 3.10 Workshops & Events
1. Open **Workshops** or **Events**.
2. Browse upcoming sessions.
3. View details for each.

### 3.11 Forms
1. Open **Forms**.
2. Same as student forms — fill and submit any assigned form.

### 3.12 ID Card & Certificates
1. Open **ID Card** — view/download your volunteer ID card.
2. Open **Certificates** — view/download certificates issued to you.

### 3.13 Profile
1. Open **Profile**, update your details, save.

## 5. Admin — Step by Step

Admin dashboard is the control center. Sections below match the sidebar.

### 5.1 Tours
1. Open **Tours** → **New Tour**.
2. Fill in tour name, dates, description, eligibility criteria.
3. Save — tour becomes visible to students.
4. Click any tour to view/manage applicants and details.

### 5.2 Tests
1. Open **Tests** → **New Test** (or start from **Templates**).
2. Add questions: MCQ, multi-select, or subjective.
3. Mark correct answers for objective questions.
4. Save and publish — link it to a tour.
5. Open a test to **Edit** it, or open individual submissions to grade subjective answers.

### 5.3 Forms
1. Open **Forms** → **New Form** (or use **Templates**).
2. Add fields (text, textarea, number, select, checkbox, radio, date, file, image).
3. Mark required fields.
4. Save and publish.
5. Open **Submissions** on any form to review what users submitted.
6. Use **Edit** to update a form later.

### 5.4 Students
1. Open **Students** to see all registered students.
2. Click a student to view their applications, test scores, and forms.

### 5.5 Volunteers
1. Open **Volunteers** to see all volunteers.
2. Click a volunteer to view their assignments, logs, expenses, and reports.

### 5.6 Groups
1. Open **Groups** → **New Group**.
2. Name the group and assign volunteers/students to it.
3. Click a group to manage members.

### 5.7 Tour Reports
1. Open **Tour Reports** to review all submitted end-of-tour reports.

### 5.8 Demo Evaluations
1. Open **Demo Evaluations** → **New** to create an evaluation.
2. Assign it to volunteers; review submitted results.

### 5.9 Finance
1. Open **Finance** to review volunteer expenses and overall tour costs.

### 5.10 Registration Fees
1. Open **Registration Fees** → **New** to record/create a fee entry.
2. Track payment status per student/volunteer.

### 5.11 Travel
1. Open **Travel** → **New** to log travel arrangements.
2. Review submitted travel details from volunteers.

### 5.12 Kits
1. Open **Kits** to manage tour kit inventory/distribution.

### 5.13 ID Cards
1. Open **ID Cards** → **New** to generate an ID card.
2. View/manage issued ID cards.

### 5.14 Certificates
1. Open **Certificates** → **New** to issue a certificate.
2. View/manage issued certificates.

### 5.15 Local Hosts
1. Open **Local Hosts** → **New** to add a host/family record.
2. View/manage existing hosts.

### 5.16 Workshops
1. Open **Workshops** → **New Workshop**.
2. Fill in details and save.
3. Click a workshop to **Edit** it.

### 5.17 Events
1. Open **Events** → **New Event**.
2. Fill in details and save.
3. Click an event to **Edit** it.

### 5.18 Visits
1. Open **Visits** → **New** to log a visit.
2. View past visits.

### 5.19 Media & Gallery
1. Open **Media** to review uploaded tour media.
2. Open **Gallery** → **New Category**, then add images inside a category for the public gallery.

### 5.20 Alumni
1. Open **Alumni** to manage alumni records.

### 5.21 Testimonials
1. Open **Testimonials** to review and publish testimonials for the public site.

### 5.22 Sponsors
1. Open **Sponsors** to manage sponsor listings.

### 5.23 Careers
1. Open **Careers** to manage job/volunteer opening listings.

### 5.24 Blog
1. Open **Blog** → **New Post**.
2. Write content, save/publish.

### 5.25 Newsletter
1. Open **Newsletter** → **New** to draft a newsletter/announcement.
2. Send/publish it.

### 5.26 Profiles
1. Open **Profiles** to view all user profiles across roles.

### 5.27 Analytics
1. Open **Analytics** for dashboard stats: active tours, applications, test performance, rankings, etc.

### 5.28 Super Admin
Only visible to **super_admin**.
1. Open **Super Admin**.
2. Assign or change a user's role (student/volunteer/staff/admin).
3. Manage overall platform access.

---

## 6. Quick Tips

- Every page you see is limited to your role — you won't see options meant for others.
- Forms and tests look and work the same everywhere: open → fill → submit → confirmation.
- Use **New** buttons to create records; click an existing row/card to view or edit it.
- Notifications alert you to status changes (application results, form deadlines, etc.).
